Our Story

Built by People Who Lived the Problem.

YVA Law Staffing didn't start in a boardroom — it started on the floor of a California law firm. Founders Hans Esmel and Paola Delgado were working remotely when the firm began asking them to refer talent. What started as a favor quickly revealed something bigger: they had a rare ability to identify, vet, and train high-performing professionals who integrated seamlessly into demanding legal environments.

As they looked at the market, one thing stood out — competitors were charging a premium for the same quality of service that Hans and Paola knew could be delivered at a fraction of the cost. Small and mid-size law firms were being priced out of the support they needed to grow.

In 2023, they founded YVA Law Staffing with a clear mission: help law firms scale their teams without burning their budget. Operating from a physical office in the Dominican Republic, YVA now provides fully bilingual, rigorously vetted virtual professionals who work in U.S. time zones and integrate directly into your firm's workflow — not as freelancers, but as real team members.
